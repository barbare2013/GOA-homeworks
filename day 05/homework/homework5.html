sea animals

Sea animals, also known as marine animals, encompass a vast array of creatures that live in saltwater environments like oceans and seas.
These include fish, marine mammals like dolphins and whales, crustaceans such as crabs and lobsters,
and many other invertebrates like jellyfish and starfish. They are incredibly diverse, with over 260,000 known species,
and many more yet to be discovered, according to the World Wildlife Fund
